About Me

I am a Northwestern University engineering student and an avid musician. As a personal project, I am passionate about motivational journaling and studying the mind to help myself and others find peace, fulfillment, and happiness in their daily lives. This is why I created my personal website. In high school I randomly picked up a poster from a UPenn recruiting event that read in massive letters, "Don't be defined by just one thing." I kept this poster in my childhood bedroom throughout my senior year, and it has stayed there since. I firmly believe that this philosophy can lead to increased fulfillment and stimulation in all areas of one's life, which is why I have chosen to explore my college journey this way. As I search for my next job or institution for further study, I hope that my website will help those reviewing my qualifications to gain a holistic perspective, along with serving as a hub for sharing my fun spread of all that makes me who I am.
